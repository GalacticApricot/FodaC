Welcome to FodaC

At FodaC, we've been proudly delivering parcels to almost the right destination for as long as anyone can remember. Our experienced team of highly motivated drivers work around the clock to ensure your "Sorry we missed you" card arrives on time.

Whether it's Cheap Chinese Bullshit or something even less important, we always take the same amount of time to arrive.

About Us

Founded in 1475, FodaC has grown into one of the most recognisable trucking companies in the virtual world. Our drivers are trained using our revolutionary "Close Enough" delivery system, allowing us to maintain our industry-leading success rate of taking pictures of people's doors and saying we tried our best to deliver.

We've built our reputation through hard work, unpaid overtime, and cheap labour. And we couldn't be prouder.

Our Mission
"Deliver cargo, break-check drivers for free insurance claims, and ensure every customer has a story to tell."

At FodaC, we believe logistics isn't about getting it perfect — it's about getting there eventually. Our mission is simple, our commitment is vague, and our delivery windows are best described as optimistic.
